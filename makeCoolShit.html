The Sxlar Wasteland Resources Documentation

Sxlar Wasteland is a creative collective with a community made up of artists, musicians, developers, and creatives of all types. We are building a community of creatives to help build networks and setup opportunities for more artists to work together.

Our mission statement is to enable as many people to make cool things as possible. To put it bluntly: the more cool shit that exists, the more cool shit there is to do/watch/learn.

Join our community and grow your network and your skills and take the first steps towards making that thing you always wanted to.

Who is Kyphxr?

A 25 year old musician, digital artist, and game dev, Kyphxr is the face of Sxlar Wasteland. He began taking piano lessons at the age of 4, and continued up until 17 where he continued to play afterwards, teaching himself guitar, music production, and minoring in sound design in college. With a major in Digital art and another minor in film, Kyphxr is our lead writer and creative director for our games studio HellStorm Software. On top of this he is the lead game dev and social media manager for the whole of the collective.
He is the creative director of our upcoming Offline MMO: Genestealer, which will be releasing sometime in 2025, specializing in creature design, he created the logo for the game [pictured below] as well as most of the horror imagery that can be found in game. Having been into hacking and puzzles his whole life, Kyphxr is also the lead designer and developer for our absurdist puzzle game:Court of the Laughing Gojo.
